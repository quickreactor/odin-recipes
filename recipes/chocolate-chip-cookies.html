Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These chewy and gooey chocolate chip cookies are a timeless treat perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Butter - 1 cup, softened
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Brown sugar - 1 cup, packed
  • Eggs - 2 large
  • Vanilla extract - 2 tsp
  • All-purpose flour - 3 cups
  • Baking soda - 1 tsp
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp
  • Chocolate chips - 2 cups

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter, sugar, and brown sugar.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop by spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  8. Let cool on a wire rack before serving.
