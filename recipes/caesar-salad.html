Caesar Salad

Description

A refreshing Caesar Salad made with crisp romaine lettuce, creamy dressing, and crunchy croutons, topped with freshly grated Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce - 1 head, chopped
  • Caesar dressing - 1/2 cup
  • Croutons - 1 cup
  • Parmesan cheese - 1/4 cup, grated
  • Chicken breast - optional, grilled and sliced

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce.
  2. Toss the lettuce with Caesar dressing in a large bowl.
  3. Add croutons and grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. If desired, add sliced grilled chicken on top.
  5. Serve immediately.
