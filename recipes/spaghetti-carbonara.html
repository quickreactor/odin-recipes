Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

This classic Italian pasta dish combines creamy eggs, savory pancetta, and freshly grated Parmesan for a simple yet indulgent meal.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti - 200g
  • Pancetta - 100g
  • Eggs - 2 large
  • Parmesan cheese - 50g, grated
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Black pepper - to taste
  • Salt - to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crisp. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix with grated Parmesan.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan with pancetta. Remove from heat.

  6. Season with black pepper and salt to taste. Serve immediately.
